## Module 6: OM Alumni FlyGuys Invests in the Next Generation of Drone Pilots

- Slot type: Founder Story
- Priority: High
- Audience: OM alumni, partners, workforce ecosystem
- Source state: source_needed
- Canva asset: Existing FlyGuys graphic from Mailchimp export
- CTA text: Read the full article
- CTA URL: https://dronexl.co/2026/05/15/flyguys-scholarship-second-year/

We love seeing OM alumni pave the way for the future. FlyGuys is returning for a second year as a headline partner for the Elevate Scholarship Program, teaming up with the National Center for Autonomous Technologies to fund 300 scholarships for aspiring young drone pilots.

The program removes financial friction by covering the $175 FAA Part 107 remote pilot test fee for students ages 16 to 24.

We are proud to watch this Lafayette-born startup help lead national workforce development.
